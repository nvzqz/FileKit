FKPath

public struct FKPath: StringLiteralConvertible,
                      RawRepresentable,
                      Hashable,
                      Indexable,
                      CustomStringConvertible,
                      CustomDebugStringConvertible

A representation of a filesystem path.

An FKPath instance lets you manage files in a much easier way.
